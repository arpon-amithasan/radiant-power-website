Hej, we're Radiant Power!

We turn waste into watts!

Radiant Power is a pioneering company specializing in the design, development, and production of cutting-edge Strontium-based nuclear batteries. Our advanced technology caters to prominent sectors such as microelectronics, small modular reactors (SMR), space exploration, and medical industries. Our mission is to harvest useful energy from spent nuclear fuel through our innovative battery technology, delivering decades of uninterrupted power without the need for replacements or recharging. Join us as we redefine the future of sustainable and long-lasting energy solutions.

Radiant Batteries

Betavoltaic batteries utilize the beta decay process, emitting high-energy beta particles from a radioactive source. These particles interact with a semiconductor material, such as silicon or diamond, creating electron-hole pairs and generating a current flow. These batteries have long lifespans and compact size but low power densities. They are used in applications prioritizing longevity and compactness, so our activities are focused around manufacturing Radiant batteries and Radiant battery powered sensors for space technology, SMR monitoring systems, and military electronics.
